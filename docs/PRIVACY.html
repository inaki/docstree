Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 5, 2025

Overview

Docstree is a Chrome extension that displays your Google Drive files in a tree structure. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

What Data We Access

Docstree accesses the following from your Google Drive:

  • File and folder names
  • Folder hierarchy (parent-child relationships)
  • File IDs (used to generate links to Google Drive)

We use read-only access. Docstree cannot modify, delete, or create files in your Drive.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Display your Drive files in a tree structure within the extension popup
  • Enable search and filtering of file names
  • Allow you to mark files as favorites for quick access

Data Storage

  • All data stays local on your device
  • Your Google OAuth token is stored in Chrome's secure local storage
  • Your preferences (theme, selected drive, favorites) are stored in browser local storage
  • We do NOT transmit your data to any external servers
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking

Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or transfer your data to any third parties. Period.

Permissions Explained

Permission Why We Need It
identity To authenticate you with Google and obtain access to your Drive
storage To save your preferences and auth token locally in Chrome
host_permissions (googleapis.com) To make API calls to Google Drive to fetch your file list

How to Revoke Access

You can revoke Docstree's access to your Google account at any time:

Option 1: Go to Google Account Permissions, find "Docstree", and click Remove Access.

Option 2: Open the extension menu and click "Sign out".

Option 3: Right-click the extension icon and select "Remove from Chrome".

Data Retention

  • We retain no data on external servers (because we don't have any)
  • Local data is cleared when you sign out or uninstall the extension
  • Favorites persist locally until you remove them or uninstall

Children's Privacy

Docstree is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
